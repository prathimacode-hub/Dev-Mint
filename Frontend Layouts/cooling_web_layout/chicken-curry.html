Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Tomatoes
  • Spices
  • Rice

Cooking Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Add chicken and cook until browned.
  3. Add tomatoes and spices, simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Serve with rice.

Nutritional Facts

Calories: 350 | Protein: 30g | Fat: 15g | Carbs: 45g
